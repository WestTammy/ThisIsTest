TVs

The best TV for you will depend on your available space and how you intend to use the set. Walmart carries a huge selection of TVs from top brands, so it's easy to find one that meets your needs.

TVs come in a wide range of sizes, from less than 20 inches to more than 60. In addition to the space where you'll put your TV, it's important to consider where you'll sit while you watch it. You'll want a larger TV if you'll be viewing it from further away and a smaller one if you'll be sitting close up.

If you're a TV or film buff, you'll probably get a lot of use out of a smart TV, which will allow you to stream content from providers like VUDU, YouTube, Netflix, Hulu and Vimeo. It will also be helpful to get a TV that offers lots of connectivity options for your Blu-ray player, Apple TV and other devices. If you're a sports fan, look for a TV that provides a wide viewing angle and offers a 1000:1 static contrast ratio for an ideal viewing experience. Gamers need TVs that can keep up with fast-paced games and have HD displays that make games look their best. For gaming, look for a TV with a response time under 6ms and a refresh rate of at least 120Hz as well as a high-resolution display.
